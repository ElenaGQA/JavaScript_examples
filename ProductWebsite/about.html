About Us

At MonoHaus, we believe that less is truly more. Rooted in simplicity and timeless design, we curate furniture that blends clean lines, natural materials, and modern elegance. Each piece is crafted to elevate your space—calm, functional, and beautiful. Welcome to living with intention.

Our philosophy centers around thoughtful living — creating spaces that feel open, peaceful, and personal. We work closely with skilled artisans and sustainable suppliers to ensure that every design not only looks good but is built to last. From the grain of the wood to the curve of a chair leg, every detail matters.

Inspired by the principles of Scandinavian and Japanese design, MonoHaus brings together form and function in perfect balance. Whether you're furnishing a single room or a whole home, our goal is to offer pieces that inspire comfort, clarity, and connection.

This is more than furniture. This is your everyday environment — made intentional.

The History of MonoHaus

MonoHaus was founded in 2015 in a small studio loft in Toronto, Canada, by two lifelong friends — a furniture designer and a woodworker — who shared a passion for timeless design and honest craftsmanship. Frustrated by the rise of disposable, mass-produced furniture, they envisioned a different kind of brand: one that combined minimalist aesthetics with durable, handcrafted quality.

Starting with just three original designs — a coffee table, a lounge chair, and a shelving unit — MonoHaus quickly gained a following through word of mouth and pop-up markets. By 2017, the team expanded to include local artisans and partnered with sustainable wood suppliers across Ontario. Every piece was made with care, reflecting a deep respect for material, form, and function.

In 2019, MonoHaus opened its first brick-and-mortar showroom, designed as a calm, gallery-like space where customers could experience the furniture as it was meant to be: warm, welcoming, and quietly beautiful. The brand's signature style — a blend of Scandinavian minimalism and Japanese-inspired simplicity — drew in homeowners, interior designers, and boutique hotel curators alike.

Despite global challenges in 2020, MonoHaus committed to staying small-batch and locally made, launching its online store and introducing customizable pieces while remaining rooted in its founding values. Today, MonoHaus is proud to serve customers across Canada and beyond, offering furniture that’s not just seen, but felt — where comfort meets craftsmanship.
